Baked Ziti with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • Two cups of penne pasta
  • One cup of parmesan or asiago cheese
  • One cup of mozarella cheese
  • One 15 oz jar of marinara sauce
  • One tablespoon of Italian or pasta seasoning
  • Two tablespoons of olive oil
  • 10 oz of baby bella mushrooms
  • One carrot
  • One clove of garlic, pressed
  • 5 oz of spinach

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta until just al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Wash and chop the mushrooms and carrot.
  3. Chop the spinach into small julienne-style strips.
  4. In a medium-size saute pan, heat the oil over medium heat until it shimmers.
  5. Saute the carrot until slightly softened.
  6. Add the mushrooms and cook until brown at the edges, about five minutes.
  7. Add a pinch of salt to the pan and stir for another 30 seconds.
  8. Turn off the heat, add the spinach and garlic and mix in.
  9. Once spinach wilts, add the marinara sauce and seasoning.
  10. Add the cooked pasta.
  11. Place the pasta in an oven-safe dish and cover with grated cheese.
  12. Heat the broiler to 550 degrees
  13. Place pasta dish on top rack, not too close to the broiler.
  14. Broil for three minutes, keeping a close eye on the cheese.
  15. Once the cheese in bubbly and golden brown, remove dish from the oven.
  16. Serve and enjoy!
